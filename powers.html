All Powers

This page contains a comprehensive list of powers featured in the Limitless universe and its inspirations. Powers may originate from original characters, external media, or conceptual toolsets.

  • Memory Anchoring: Retaining knowledge across timelines. (Coil)

  • Disruption Pulse: Emitting shockwaves that interfere with tech or focus. (Blaze)
  • Bitshift: Short-range reality edits and spatial warping. (Jaxbit)
  • Playback Loop: Replaying the last 5 seconds of actions. (Jaxbit)

  • Fragment Surge: Weaponizing fractured space. (Elliot)
  • Echo Constructs: Creating unstable timeline copies. (Elliot)
  • Emotional Detonation: Feeding off strong emotions to enhance power. (Elliot)
