About the Project

Limitless: The Ultimate Strategy is a world-hopping, multiversal story about heroes, survival, and power. It features Coil, Blue, Red, and many others in a battle across infinite realities. This wiki serves as a complete guide to the lore, powers, and history of Limitless—including spinoffs like TINPOTS UNITE!
