Basic Info

  • Name: Blue
  • Age: 22
  • Gender: He/Him
  • Role: Core Protagonist, Combat Medic, Close Ally of Coil
  • First Appearance: Limitless: The Ultimate Strategy

Appearance

Blue has white skin, a sturdy build, and wears a tin pot as a hat. His messy white tuft of hair sticks out from beneath the pot. He wears a grey hoodie with a tin pot decal on it, black pants, and headphones around his neck. A bandage wraps around his right arm—likely from battle wounds. His expression is usually a relaxed -_-, but don't let that fool you—he's full of energy.

Personality

Blue is fun-loving, loyal, and always ready to help his team. He’s the kind of guy who’d carry you out of a burning timeline with a smile and a “wuh- that was close! :P”. He often acts as the group's morale booster, cracking jokes and staying cool under pressure. Though chill and relaxed on the outside, he's got a huge heart and will stand by his friends no matter what.

Talking Style

Blue talks super casually, often using slang and tons of emoticons. Common phrases include:

  • “ayyy that was sick :D”
  • “wuh- i didn’t even see that coming”
  • “A what the heck was that”
  • “yea yea i got this :P”

He’s a goofball in the best way, lightening up even the heaviest moments.

Skills & Abilities

  • Wallhopping: Blue is exceptionally skilled at scaling and maneuvering walls in combat environments.
  • First Aid: He's a talented combat medic and can survive extreme conditions, including nearly drowning for an hour.
  • Luck: Events tend to go in his favor... even when they really shouldn’t.

Relationships

  • Coil: Blue's best friend and longest-standing ally. Their bond is full of good-natured teasing and shared history. They trust each other with their lives.
  • Red: They have mutual respect and often team up on operations. Blue says her food "goes crazy :D".
  • Dylan: Blue likes poking fun at Dylan’s temper, but genuinely respects his strength and bravery.
  • The Tinpots: Blue wears a Tinpot hat proudly and often hangs out with them in his free time.

Trivia

  • He once joked that his tin pot “makes him lucky” and now refuses to take it off.
  • Blue can swim for hours without issue and often rescues others from underwater missions.
  • He always seems to have a backup plan—or just really good timing.
