Basic Info

  • Name: Rochas313
  • Age: Approximately 22
  • Gender: Male
  • Appearance: Green jacket, green skater hat, white shirt, black pants
  • Affiliation: Nosoi (The Tree Virus)

Overview

Rochas313 is a Roblox game developer turned infected entity from the SPREAD ARG. Originally passionate and creative, his world was overtaken by the parasitic tree virus known as Nosoi, transforming him into a vessel for its dark will. He plays a significant role as both creator and corrupted force within his digital universe.

Origins

Rochas313 was a talented Roblox developer collaborating closely with friends like cappy0. His creations were gradually corrupted when Nosoi infiltrated his game, spreading its parasitic roots throughout the Roblox platform and infecting digital worlds.

Transformation & Infection

  • Nosoi’s influence blurred his identity, turning him into an unwilling host.
  • Wooden branches and roots grew from his body, symbolizing infection.
  • His avatar’s colors and attire became corrupted and tainted by the virus.

Abilities

  • Computer Manipulation: Controls computer code to force blue screens and prevent deletion.
  • Game Infection: Spreads Nosoi by infecting Roblox games with growing parasitic trees.
  • NPC Control: Manipulates NPCs by linking roots to them, speaking through their voices.
  • Cloning: Creates duplicates of individuals to spread further infection.

Role in Forsaken

In the game Forsaken, Rochas313 appears as a powerful antagonist whose Nosoi powers challenge players. Despite his corruption, remnants of his former self occasionally emerge, hinting at the person he once was.
