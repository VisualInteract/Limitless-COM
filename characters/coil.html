Character Profile: Coil

Home Characters Powers Universes Timelines Factions Spinoffs Lore

Basic Info

  • Name: Coil
  • Age: 29
  • Role: Protagonist, Multiversal Creator
  • First Appearance: Limitless

Overview

Coil is the central figure of the Limitless universe. A survivor of a multiversal war, he carries the burden of witnessing countless versions of his friends die. As the creator of this universe, he uses his powers not to control, but to protect and rebuild what was lost. His journey is deeply tied to memory, grief, and rebuilding trust—especially with those closest to him.

Personality

Coil is resilient, emotionally complex, and fiercely loyal to his found family. He has a cynical view of traditional family structures—believing "family is just another thing to backstab you"—but holds deep trust in his friends like Blue, Red, Dylan, and others. He hides his pain well and often masks vulnerability with determination or silence. He can be emotionally distant at times, but his core is protective and warm.

Powers & Abilities

  • Creation: Coil has the power to create and shape universes.
  • Memory Anchoring: He retains knowledge across timelines and universes, helping him prevent past mistakes.
  • Reality Awareness: Coil can perceive cross-universal events and temporal shifts.

Coil's abilities are not about flashy combat but instead represent narrative control, resistance to cosmic corruption, and immense multiversal knowledge.

Relationships

  • Blue: A close friend with a strained yet deeply rooted bond.
  • Red: Loyal and steady, Red is someone Coil trusts deeply. Her calmness contrasts with his inner turmoil.
  • Dylan: A version of himself—angrier and more emotional—but still a protector at heart.
  • Jaxbit: Chaotic and reckless, Jaxbit brings unpredictability to Coil's universe. Their relationship is tense but interdependent.

Trivia

  • Coil wears a Tinpot hat sometimes and likes how it looks.
  • He overcame survivor’s guilt after realizing his fallen friends hold no anger toward him in this timeline.
  • He is based on the player character of the creator of the wiki.
